IMPORTANT: Files you upload to our tools are processed by AI for the sole purpose of generating your requested analysis. We do not use your uploaded business data to train AI models, share it with third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond your requested analysis.

Uploaded files are:

  • Not shared with any third parties except our AI processing providers (OpenAI) as required to generate your analysis.

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.
